Help Center

Frequently Asked
Questions

Quick answers for students and staff.

Who can use CampusFind?+
CampusFind is exclusively for current students, faculty, and staff. You must register with your name, roll number, department, and a valid college email address.
Where are the campus drop-off points?+
There are 5 official drop-off points: Main Library (Ground Floor), Student Cafeteria (Reception Desk), Sports Complex (Entry Gate), Admin Block (Room 101), and the Main Security Office. Open weekdays 8 AM – 6 PM.
Why is my report showing as Pending?+
All new reports go through a brief admin review before appearing in Browse. This usually takes a short while. You can track your report status in the My Items section of your dashboard.
How long are items kept at drop-off points?+
Items are held for 30 days. Electronics and ID documents are kept for 60 days. Unclaimed items are donated to the campus charity drive.
Is my personal data visible to others?+
No. Your contact details are never shown on public listings. They are only shared after staff verify your ownership claim.
Can I edit or delete my report?+
Yes. Go to My Items in your dashboard to edit details or delete a report. Editing a report resets it to Pending for re-review by admin.
